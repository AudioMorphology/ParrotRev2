\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
union of a float and a 32-\/Bit integer 

The elements of the Union can be accessed separately even though they are the {\itshape same} 4 x 8-\/bit bytes. This enables a Float to be passed about for processing, but stored and read from RSRAM as if it were a 32-\/Bit word
